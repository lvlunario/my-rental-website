Your Private Resort in Ilocos Norte

Welcome to Casa Deoasay, your exclusive gateway to the stunning beauty of Northern Luzon! This spacious and welcoming home is the perfect venue for your next family vacation, reunion, or special event. With the capacity to sleep up to 24 people, our property offers a unique blend of comfort, convenience, and celebration. Whether you're booking the entire home for a large group, renting the function hall for a wedding or birthday, or simply looking for a relaxing day with access to our private pool, Casa Deoasay is your perfect destination.

The Space

Our home features 4 large, air-conditioned bedrooms designed for comfort and relaxation. The open-plan living and dining areas provide ample space for everyone to gather, while the fully equipped kitchen allows you to prepare your own meals. Step outside to enjoy our pristine swimming pool and a dedicated function hall perfect for creating lasting memories.

Guest Access & Neighborhood

Conveniently located in Napu, Badoc, the property is your ideal home base for exploring the rich culture and natural wonders of the Ilocos region. We are just a short drive from the historic city of Vigan, the vibrant city of Laoag, and the famous white sand beaches of Pagudpud. The property is easily accessible and offers ample, secure parking for your vehicles.
