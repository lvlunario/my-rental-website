About the Property

Experience one of the nicest units in South Kihei after its brand new renovation in April 2019! This beautiful 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom ground floor corner unit is perfectly situated close to the pool and hot tub, and just a short walk from the world-famous Charley Young and Kamaole I Beaches. With restaurants, shops, and services all nearby, you'd be hard-pressed to find a better value on Maui. Enjoy brand new black stainless Samsung appliances, including an in-unit Washer/Dryer, for your convenience.

The Space

Our unique "floating" beds with 14" memory foam mattresses practically guarantee you'll sleep like a baby after an epic day of beach hopping, hiking, or exploring the Valley Isle. The open living area comfortably accommodates four adults and features a large 4K UHD LG TV with free cable and WiFi, ensuring that even a rare rainy day is a comfortable one.

The Neighborhood

This condo is in the perfect location. You are right across the street from incredible beaches, a short drive from the exclusive Wailea area, and within walking distance to fantastic restaurants, live music, nightlife, and shopping. Discover why guests return to this spot year after year!
