Your Oceanfront Home Base

We are delighted you're considering our condo! As a local family living on the island, we've designed this space to be the perfect, comfortable home base for your Maui adventures. Enjoy stunning ocean views from your private lanai, where you can watch the boats in Maalaea Harbor and even spot surfers on windy days. The condo is centrally located, making it incredibly convenient to explore every corner of the island.

The Neighborhood

You're just a short 5-minute walk from the Maui Ocean Center, Maalaea Harbor (where many boat tours depart), and a variety of shops and restaurants. For family fun, the Maui Golf & Sports Park is also within walking distance. If you love long walks on the beach, the beginning of Sugar Beach—the longest sandy beach in Maui—is right nearby, perfect for morning strolls and watching the sunset.

Our Local Recommendations

As locals, we love sharing our favorite spots! Here are a few must-try places and activities, taken from our personal guide.

Popular Maui Eats

  • Geste Shrimp Food Truck: A local favorite at Kahului Harbor.
  • 808 Grindz: Famous for their Macadamia Nut Pancakes.
  • Mama's Fish House: Pricey, but an unforgettable dining experience.
  • Leoda's: Incredible sandwiches and pies.

Must-Do Activities

  • Road to Hana: An all-day adventure. Bring cash!
  • Haleakala Sunrise/Sunset: Sunrise requires a reservation.
  • Sugar Beach: Walk for miles on the longest beach in Maui.
  • Maui Swap Meet: The best spot for souvenirs every Saturday morning.
